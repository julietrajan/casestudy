# 🧩 Executive Simulation Activity  

### Using AI Language Service in Customer Intelligence

---

## 🎯 Business Scenario

You are the **Leadership Team of a Global Electronics Manufacturer** operating across:

- 🇮🇳 India  
- 🇩🇪 Germany  
- 🇧🇷 Brazil  
- 🇯🇵 Japan  

Over the past **6 months**:

- Customer churn in your **premium segment** has increased by **12%**
- Support tickets increased by **27%**
- Product return rates have **doubled in LATAM**

### You receive:

- 📧 18,000 customer support emails / month  
- ⭐ 40,000 product reviews across marketplaces  
- 💬 12,000 chatbot conversations  
- 🛠️ 3,500 service engineer notes  
- 🌐 Feedback in **6 languages across geographies**

> ⚠️ Your teams are currently sampling only **~2%** of this feedback manually.

---

## 🔍 The Executive Dilemma

Your COO asks:

> *“We already use dashboards and sentiment reports. Why do we need AI Language Service? Can it do something that BI tools can't?”*

You now have the opportunity to deploy:

- ✅ Sentiment Analysis  
- ✅ Key Phrase Extraction  
- ✅ Named Entity Recognition  
- ✅ Conversational PII Detection  
- ✅ Opinion Mining  
- ✅ Text Summarization  
- ✅ Custom Classification Models  

---

## 📁 Dataset Sample

### 📧 Email – Germany
> "The new smart thermostat adjusts temperature too frequently at night. I woke up twice because it suddenly turned off heating."

---

### 💬 Chat Transcript – India
> "Installation technician didn't explain the mobile app setup."

---

### ⭐ Product Review – Brazil
> "Voice assistant doesn't recognize Portuguese commands properly."

---

### 🛠️ Service Engineer Notes – Japan
> "Customer mentioned firmware update caused remote connectivity failure."

---

## 🧩 The Executive Challenge

Using **AI Language Service capabilities**, answer the following:

---

### ✅ Task 1: Insight Discovery

Which of the following can you extract automatically?

- Product‑specific dissatisfaction trends  
- Feature‑level sentiment  
- Region‑wise usability issues  
- Installation vs software usage complaints  
- Regulatory risk (health / safety concerns)

🎯 **Executives decide:**

➡️ Which AI capability will help uncover this?  
*(e.g., Opinion Mining vs Custom Classification)*

---


### ✅ Task 2: Risk Detection

Your compliance team notices some chats say:

> *“Thermostat shut down overnight. Room temperature dropped below safe level.”*

Discuss:

- Can Language AI proactively detect liability risk?  
- Which capability would identify:
  - Safety incidents?
  - Warranty claims?
  - Personally Identifiable Information (PII)?

---

### ✅ Task 3: Executive Outcome

Pick **one measurable KPI improvement**:

- Reduce Return Rate  
- Reduce Support Calls  
- Improve First‑Time Install Success  
- Increase Premium Renewal  

🎯 **Executives must articulate:**

> *If we deploy Language AI for ___ insight extraction, we expect to improve ___ by ___% because ___.*
